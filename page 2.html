My GrandFather




My GrandFather was Born on April 5th 1945, his name is also Patrick Leavitt. He served in Vietnam for 5 years and was a Sargent. He then pursued a construction business which he owned and operated for 20 years before selling out and retiring. He has been a very influential person in everything that I Have done and is a key part of who I am.
